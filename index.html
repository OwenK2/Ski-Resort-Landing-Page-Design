Fresh Snow

Ice peak is proud of its prime location where snow continuously falls. We have fresh powder all year round perfect for professionals and first timers, who might need the extra cushion.

Free Transportation

No need to worry about getting around. At Ice Peak we provide completely free transportation so you can get from your room to the slopes as quick and cleanly as possible

Amazing Restraunts

During your stay you will have several options to eat at any of our restraunts. Voted the best on Mars by the Martian Restraunt Committie our restraunts are affordable and exclusive for guests only.
